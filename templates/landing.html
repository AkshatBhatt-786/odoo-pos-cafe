End-to-end Workflow

From table to
receipt, seamlessly.

Step 01
Open Session

Staff logs in and opens a POS session. The terminal is ready immediately.

Step 02
Select Table

Pick a table from the floor view. Order creation starts instantly.

Step 03
Add Items

Browse products by category, add to cart, adjust quantities with +/−.

Step 04
Send to Kitchen

Hit Send — the KDS receives the ticket instantly for preparation.

Step 05
Complete Payment

Choose Cash, Card, or UPI QR. Confirm and the table resets automatically.
